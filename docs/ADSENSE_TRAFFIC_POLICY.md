# AdSense traffic and interaction policy

This policy applies before and after AdSense activation.

- The owner, developers, editors, contractors, friends, and testers must not click live ads or repeatedly load pages to create impressions.
- Never ask visitors to click, view, or refresh ads to support the site.
- Do not buy paid-to-click, paid-to-view, auto-surf, bot, click-exchange, or incentivized traffic.
- Do not promote the site through spam email, spam posts, browser toolbars, unwanted redirects, popups, or software-driven traffic.
- Review every paid campaign, affiliate, agency, or traffic partner before it sends users to ad-bearing pages.
- Do not test live ads by clicking them. Use AdSense reporting and approved preview or testing methods.
- Do not modify AdSense code to inflate performance or hide its origin.
- Do not place ads in email, private communications, popups, popunders, software, framed third-party content, ad-only pages, or pages without substantial publisher content.

The repository keeps advertising disabled until the AdSense site is Ready, consent management is configured, and all owner traffic confirmations are recorded.
